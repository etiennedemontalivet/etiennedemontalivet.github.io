1-day formation

For 2 years I gave a 1-day introduction to AI in Industry to leaders of research and innovation departments. The objective was to make them aware of the common AI project timelines, needed resources, the common mistakes and of course, some insights of the machine learning algorithms, from the data collection to the model evaluation.

We had very positive and enthousiastic feedbacks from all participants, from the clarity of the speech to the theoretical explainations and practical exercises.
